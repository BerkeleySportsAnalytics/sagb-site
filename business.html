About the Business Branch

Every semester, our Business Branch collaborates with sports teams and client on projects regarding marketing, partnerships, ticketing, and other facets of the sports business industry. Below are some of our past projects. 

Boston Red Sox Sponsorships

Summary: Last semester, the Business team worked with the Boston Red Sox on creating new unique sponsorship opportunities outside of sports, which included making prospective deal valuations and value alignment.

Oklahoma City Dodgers Marketing

Summary: This project was focused on improving the email marketing for the Oklahoma City Dodgers (Triple-A Dodgers) and included diving into their internal data as well as analyzing demographics and other trends to maximize their email interactions.

Nashville Sounds Sports Betting Implementation

Summary: One of the most fun projects the Business branch has had was helping the Nashville Sounds (Triple-A Brewers) implement sports betting into their stadium right after it was legalized in Tennessee, which made for a ton of interesting research and unique perspectives.

San Jose Earthquakes Concessions Pricing Strategy

Abstract: This case analyzes the concession pricing for the San Jose Earthquakes, and gives recommendations as to how the pricing schemes can be better. The analysis includes how to best align the pricing such that the team profits from their concessions. This project was done as a consulting project with the San Jose Earthquakes.
