What we do

Sports Analytics Group at Berkeley aims to give members a full experience doing real sports analytics via consulting and research.

We are a student-run sports analytics research group at UC Berkeley. Our organization consists of former sports analytics interns for the Brooklyn Nets, Milwaukee Brewers, New York Mets, and Adidas. Our backgrounds vary from Statistics, Computer Science, Physics, and Economics undergraduate and graduate students at UC Berkeley. Learn more here

Each team focuses on different aspects of sports analytics. Our projects team focuses on consulting for sports teams, and long term research projects. The data journalism team creates short-form articles that are grounded in data and data visualization. The business team gives members experience with hosting speaker events, industry panels, and more!

Blog

Every so often, we like to publish interesting articles that's current and controversial in sports. We've published articles about cheerleading, esports, and kneeling during the anthem. Head over to the blog to learn more!
