Main Purpose

  • Educating members to become industry leaders in the sports analytics field
  • Research teams to further develop analytical skills and apply relevant statistics to sports-related problems
  • Partnering with Cal Athletics and Professional Sports Teams as a consulting group to provide data driven sports analysis.
  • Opportunities to compete in relevant case competitions at Sloan and SABR
  • Connecting members with alumni and industry leaders in front offices
  • Hosting industry panels, with an opportunity to network with people doing sports analytics as a career

Our Teams

Projects

This committee aims to do research projects that are either self directed or consulting projects with professional sports teams. Work will be done in teams four and will be typically be based around a single topic/teams questions. So far we have gotten projects from the San Jose Earthquakes about concession pricing and about the most effective way to gain fans. We are also reaching out to teams in the NBA, MLB, NFL and various minor league sports to get more projects. Priority will be given to people with a strong CS/Stats background, especially people that know anything about machine learning, although these are not the only skills applicants will be judged by.

Data Journalism

This committee is about coming together to discuss trending topics in the sports world with peers; and then making bold, and impartial, statements or predictions using evidence supported via analytics and statistics. You can write about any sport with data, from Basketball to Baseball to Pigeon Racing! For examples of past articles written by members of this team, check out http://berkeleysportsanalytics.org/data-journalism-basketball.

Business

This team will be in charge of the business and marketing side of the club. Overall, they will be managing events and finances. The team will be split up into the following categories

  • External Affairs (Case Competition, Industry Networking Night, Events open to the public)
  • Social Media (In charge of Twitter, Facebook, and such)
  • Fundraising (Raise funds for the club)
